### natural capital ensurance

insurance pays you *after* the damage. **ensurance funds the conditions that prevent it** — proactive protection for ecosystems, species, and natural capital. ex nunc, not ex post.

we make nature's value legible to capital so capital can protect it. naturalizing finance, not financializing nature. the price is a bridge — never the claim that a dollar figure *is* the worth.

onchain on Base. built as a protocol: agents (place · people · purpose), certificates, coins, and proceeds that route value toward what matters.

### what we're building

an entire industry — the way insurance grew from a coffee house into instruments, actors, markets, and a profession. ensurance is the proactive counterpart, and we're its first participant.

- **valuation** — RealValue: ecosystem-service value over the stocks & flows of natural capital
- **instruments** — certificates & coins, 1:1 with the real things they protect
- **agents** — onchain identities for the places, people, and purposes worth ensuring
- **markets** — for what matters
